### New Open Dataset Published: Longitudinal dataset (2000-2026) on the density of mental health and neurology specialists per 100,000 inhabitants in 16 Ibero-American countries, including OECD and LAC averages. Variables: psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, neurologists, neurop

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Longitudinal dataset (2000-2026) on the density of mental health and neurology specialists per 100,000 inhabitants in 16 Ibero-American countries, including OECD and LAC averages. Variables: psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, neurologists, neurop*.

The dataset is freely available under the **CC0 1.0 Universal** license, allowing unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction.
