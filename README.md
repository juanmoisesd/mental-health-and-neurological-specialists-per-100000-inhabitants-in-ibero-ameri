## Overview
Longitudinal dataset (2000-2026) on the density of mental health and neurology specialists per 100,000 inhabitants in 16 Ibero-American countries, including OECD and LAC averages. Variables: psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, neurologists, neuropsychiatrists, mental health nurses, social workers, total workforce. Sources: WHO Mental Health Atlas 2001-2020; PAHO projections 2026.
